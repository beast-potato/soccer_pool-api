General Info

  • Login/Singup using your @plasticmobile email
  • The Cup tab shows the status of upcoming games and allows you to manage game predictions
  • The Pool tab shows you how many points each player in the pool has
  • Buy in is $10 and it's winner take all
  • In the event of a tie for first place, the pool will be distributed evenly to all players with the highest score
  • Prediction window closes 30 mins before a game starts

Scoring

Each game prediction earns you points according to the following rules

  • Predict the winner +2
  • Predict a teams score +1
  • A perfect prediction will earn 5
